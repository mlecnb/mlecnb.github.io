Home Learning

What is Home Learning?

Home Learning is exactly that--learning at home! The possibilities are endless. On this page, you will find a quarterly Home Learning list, full of ideas that connect to the content being studied in class. You will also find a collection of links to educational websites to visit. The list does not stop there. I encourage your child to get creative. If he/she completes any kind of Home Learning, use the student calendar to sign, or even send a note or email.

When is Home Learning collected?

Home Learning is checked every Friday morning.

Is there a grade or incentive?

Home Learning is not reported on our report card, but we teach our students that learning is all around us, regardless of grades. If the student has completed at least 3 Home Learning tasks, they are invited to our weekly Lunch Bunch Celebration in the classroom!
