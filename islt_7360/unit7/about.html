About Mrs. Pudenz

Hello! I am Megan Pudenz and I’m second grade teacher at English Landing Elementary. This is my seventh year as a teacher! This career path was an easy choice, as I’ve known I wanted to be a teacher since I was an elementary student with a pretend classroom in my basement. I grew up in Kansas City, Missouri where I attended the Park Hill School District, who is now my employer. I am a Missouri Tiger at heart, as I am currently working on my third degree from the University of Missouri-Columbia. My degrees are:

  • Bachelor’s of Science in Elementary Education (2013)
  • Master’s of Science in Curriculum and Instruction (2014)
  • Education Specialist in Technology in Schools (expected Summer 2020)

My husband and I married in 2014 and welcomed our son, Bennett, in January 2018. Besides spending time with my family, I also enjoy exploring new recipes to cook, refinishing furniture or building new pieces with my husband, and spending time outside. Some of my favorite things include Chipotle, taking a walk, HGTV shows, and decorating. In my classroom, I encourage my students to work hard and respect others. My favorite moments are when a student has a “lightbulb moment” where a concept clicked for them for the first time. Their smiles are contagious! Our class Mission Statement is:

“The leaders of Room 210 will come prepared to learn and have fun. We work together to listen, help others, show respect, and have positive attitudes.”
